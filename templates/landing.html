About Baptist Student Fellowship

The Baptist Student Fellowship (BSF) is a vibrant community of past and present students united in faith and fellowship. Established in 1975 under the Nigerian Baptist Convention at The Federal Polytechnic Ilaro, BSF has been a beacon of spiritual growth, service, and camaraderie.

Our mission is to nurture spiritual development, foster meaningful relationships, and empower members to impact their communities positively.
